Are corona restrictions being eased too rapidly?

In many European countries including Italy, Spain and Hungary lockdown restrictions have already been relaxed, and more are set to follow suit in the coming weeks. Shops have reopened in many places and there are less restrictions on going outside. Commentators fear a second wave of infections - but for some the return to normality can't happen fast enough.
